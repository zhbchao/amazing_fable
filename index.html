Fable 5 · A working showcase

Everything here was imagined, designed, and coded by a machine.

Ten interactive pieces — a quarter-million-particle universe, a fluid you can pour with your cursor, a neural network learning live in your tab, an endless symphony — built end to end by Claude Fable 5. Every file, every shader, every note. Click anything.

Three.js / WebGL Web Audio Canvas 2D / SVG June 2026

  1. 01

    Genesis

    220,000 GPU particles breathing between a spiral galaxy, Saturn, a strange attractor, DNA, and type — simplex turbulence, bloom, and a cursor that stirs the dust.

     Three.js · GPU →
  2. 02

    Ink

    A real Navier–Stokes solver in WebGL: drag to pour luminous ink and watch vorticity curl it into storms — velocity, pressure, and dye fields computed every frame.

     Fluid dynamics · GPU →
  3. 03

    Watch It Learn

    A neural network — backprop and Adam, from scratch — trains in your tab to paint a picture. Doodle your own and it learns that instead.

     Machine learning →
  4. 04

    Endless Symphony

    An infinite composition improvised from raw oscillators — chords, bass, melody, drums. No samples, no loops, never the same twice.

     Generative music →
  5. 05

    Sudden Death

    New

    A penalty shootout under the floodlights — drag through the ball, bend it past a keeper who reads your habits, then pull on the gloves yourself. Best of five.

     Game dev · Three.js →
  6. 06

    Neon Breakout

    A complete arcade game: particle explosions, screen shake, combo scoring, and a synthesized soundtrack — from a single prompt.

     Game dev →
  7. 07

    Tearable Cloth

    Verlet-integration cloth you can grab, fling, and rip apart — with wind, gravity, and stress-colored fibers.

     Physics →
  8. 08

    Flow Fields

    Thousands of particles drift through a hand-rolled noise field, painting a unique artwork in real time. Curated palettes, PNG export.

     Generative art →
  9. 09

    Infinite Landscapes

    A procedural landscape generator: every click composes a new animated scene — sky, light, ridgelines, wildlife — downloadable as clean SVG.

     SVG generation →
  10. 10

    Restless Earth

    Every earthquake on the planet from the last 30 days, pulled live from the USGS and rendered on a wireframe globe — press play and the whole month replays.

     Data viz · Live feed →
